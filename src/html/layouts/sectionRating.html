How we make a rating

Having selected the most popular applications from bookmakers in India, we compile a list of key parameters. These are the characteristics that our users would consider to be the best betting app. So, among the parameters we can note:

  1. Application reliability. Reliability when searching for software to download to a smartphone, it is important to comply with increased data protection standards.
  2. Functionality. The presence of basic (pre-match and live line) and additional (Fast Games, Table Slots, Crash-Games) sections in the application.
  3. Interface. Intuitive navigation. Main menu: registration, login, sports line, auxiliary menu: validator for license verification, chat with support, personal account sections, including bonuses, cash register and tournaments.
  4. Betting market range. The presence of a wide betting line at the bookmaker itself also affects the final rating of the application.
